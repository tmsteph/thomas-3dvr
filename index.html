WHO I AM?

My name is Thomas M. Stephens Jr. I've been collecting knowlege all over the internet and world.This is somewhat of a portfolio. I considered Wordpress, bootstrap, and react websites, but wanted to experiment building from scratch.

Computer Science

I've always loved exploring computers, but everyone has a had a hard time sitting down to program. There are so many options. So many ways to explore and learn. I hope that while developing this website we can hash out a path that everyone can use

Suppliments

I've found many suppliments to be helpful in my life. Like most people, I have struggled with focus, energy, anxiety, and depression. Many of these things can be relieved through spiritual excercises such as Yoga and Meditation, but it is nice to have options.

Spirituality

I have been always been fascinated with the bigger picture. Why are we here? Where did we come from? Where are we going? What can we do to increase our capacity for love and expand that to help others? Humanity has been on this planet for 250,000 years. In a universe that is 4.6 billion years old, where do we fit in? I believe that we can all learn to look outside our own desires and develop a broader awareness that can expand to reveal that we are all a part of the universal conciousness.

Finance

People have a hard time recognising that building wealth can be easy. The hardest part is transitioning from a consumer to a producer. Humans only need air, water, food, and shelter to survive.

Why are we making these things hard to obtain?

Employees give most of their production away to the employer in return for a garunteed wage. You have to treat youself like a business, investing in yourself, building your skills, and making a habit of work without immediate reward.
